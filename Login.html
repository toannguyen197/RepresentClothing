About Us

Represent Clo appeared in early 2020, Represent brings the power of a local brand full of personality with the leading fashion products. The meaning of this brand means to represent, to be a leading studio in street fashion with the creativity of leading designers. Represent will become a symbol of a strong, bold, and popular fashion style.

Bring a unique perspective not only to lifestyle but also to modern dress. The growing demand of Streetwear Vietnam market for fashion items at Represent is designed with Asian and European cultural fusion, creating a unique and new style. From black and white tones, the main color tone of most Streetwear fashion lines, the fashion models at Represent have a variety of colors. This is how Represent brings value to a young Streetwear-loving community, encouraging them to take risks in their clothing style. With a risk-free and risk-free personality, Represent gradually expanded the market during the development of branches across the country, bringing a very unique culture of Represent Studio.
